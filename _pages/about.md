# Steffen Frenzel

I am a PhD student in Computational Linguistics at the University of
Potsdam.

My research focuses on multilingual text alignment, particularly the
alignment of sentences and elementary discourse units in German–English
texts. I am interested in multilingual representation models, automatic
alignment methods, and the evaluation of alignment systems.

For further information, see my [CV](/cv/) and
[publications](/publications/).
